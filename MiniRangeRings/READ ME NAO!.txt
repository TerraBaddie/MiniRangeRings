If you want a smaller version of World of Warcraft minimap Cursor
(I just cut out the circle below the arrow)

YOU MUST CUT (CTRL + X) "MinimapArrow.blp" file and paste it into:

"WORLDOFWARCRAFT DIRECTORY LOCATION\Interface\Minimap\HERE"

Example:

"World of Warcraft Vanilla+\Interface\Minimap\MinimapArrow.blp"

Otherwise rename "MiniRangeRings-master" to "MiniRangeRings"

and stick it into: "\Interface\AddOns\"

Like so:

"\Interface\AddOns\MiniRangeRings"

if you dont like the minimap cursor edit just delete:

"\Minimap\" in Interface folder. CHEERS!
